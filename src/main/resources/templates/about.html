About István Fekete's Animal Characters

István Fekete (1900-1970)

István Fekete was a renowned Hungarian writer known for his novels depicting the natural world and the lives of animals. His works are popular in children's literature as they bring nature closer to young readers.

His animal novels not only introduce the life of animals but also demonstrate human traits and relationships on a symbolical level, making them accessible and meaningful to readers of all ages.

About This Website

Our website is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the unique animal characters created by István Fekete in his novels. We have compiled a database of these characters, including their names, species, and the novels they appear in.

This project aims to help readers discover or rediscover these beloved characters and the novels they inhabit, promoting appreciation for Fekete's contribution to literature and his portrayal of the natural world.

Database Information

Our database contains:

  • Animal characters created by István Fekete
  • Details about their species
  • The novels they appear in
  • Publication information about each novel

We constantly update our database as we research and document more of Fekete's works and the characters within them.
